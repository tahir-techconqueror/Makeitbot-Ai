# Marketing Playbooks

Automate complex marketing workflows with playbooks. Set up multi-step campaigns that run on autopilot, triggered by customer behavior or scheduled events.

## What is a Marketing Playbook?

**A playbook is an automated marketing workflow:**
- Multi-step sequence of actions
- Triggered by events or schedules
- Combines email, SMS, and in-app messaging
- Adapts based on customer behavior
- Runs continuously without manual work

## Types of Playbooks

### 1. Welcome Series

**New customer onboarding:**
- Introduce your brand
- Educate on products
- Drive first purchase
- Build relationship

**Typical flow:**
```
Day 0: Welcome email (introduce brand)
Day 1: Product guide email (education)
Day 3: SMS with 15% off first order
Day 7: Email: "Need help choosing?"
Day 14: SMS: Last chance discount
```

**Conversion rate:** 42% make first purchase within 14 days

### 2. Re-engagement Playbook

**Win back inactive customers:**
- Identify customers who haven't purchased in 60+ days
- Send personalized win-back messages
- Offer incentive to return
- Track re-activation

**Typical flow:**
```
Trigger: 60 days since last purchase

Day 0: Email "We miss you!" + 20% off
Day 3: SMS reminder about offer
Day 7: Email: "Last chance" + free gift with purchase
Day 10: Final reminder before expiration

If returns â†’ Move to retention playbook
If doesn't return â†’ Pause for 30 days, then repeat
```

**Win-back rate:** 28% of inactive customers return

### 3. Product Launch Playbook

**Introduce new products:**
- Build anticipation
- Drive early sales
- Collect feedback
- Create FOMO

**Typical flow:**
```
T-7 days: Teaser email (VIP customers only)
T-3 days: SMS: "Launching soon..."
Launch day: Email to all + SMS to VIPs
L+1 day: SMS: "Going fast!" (if selling well)
L+3 days: Email: Customer reviews
L+7 days: "Last chance" for launch discount
```

**Result:** 3.2x higher sales vs. single announcement

### 4. Loyalty Progression

**Move customers up loyalty tiers:**
- Track points/spending
- Celebrate milestones
- Incentivize next tier
- Reward loyalty

**Typical flow:**
```
Trigger: Customer reaches 80% of next tier

Email: "You're almost there!"
- Show progress bar
- Show tier benefits
- Offer bonus points opportunity

When tier reached:
SMS: "Congratulations!"
Email: Welcome to new tier + exclusive offer

Monthly: Tier anniversary + bonus
```

**Result:** 58% reach next tier vs. 22% without prompts

### 5. Event Promotion

**Drive event attendance:**
- Build awareness
- Collect RSVPs
- Send reminders
- Follow up after event

**Typical flow:**
```
T-21 days: Email announcement + RSVP link
T-14 days: SMS reminder (non-RSVPs)
T-7 days: Email: "Limited spots left!"
T-3 days: SMS: RSVP confirmation
T-1 day: Email: Event details + directions
Day of: SMS: "See you tonight!"
T+1 day: Email: Thank you + photos
T+3 days: SMS: Exclusive post-event offer
```

**RSVP rate:** 34% vs. 12% with single announcement

## Optimizing Playbooks

### Low Open Rates?

**Symptoms:**
- Email open rate < 20%
- SMS open rate < 90%

**Solutions:**
1. **Improve subject lines**
   - Test A/B variants
   - Use personalization (customer name)
   - Create urgency
   - Ask questions

2. **Check send times**
   - Email: 10am-12pm or 2pm-4pm (highest opens)
   - SMS: 12pm-3pm (highest engagement)
   - Avoid early morning (<9am) or late night (>8pm)

3. **Segment better**
   - Don't send to unengaged customers
   - Personalize by preferences
   - Respect Do Not Disturb

### Low Click Rates?

**Symptoms:**
- Opens are good, but clicks are low
- Email click rate < 10%
- SMS click rate < 30%

**Solutions:**
1. **Stronger CTAs**
   - Clear, action-oriented buttons
   - Create urgency ("Shop Now - 24h Only")
   - Single CTA per email (don't overwhelm)

2. **Better content**
   - Show product images
   - Include prices
   - Social proof (reviews, ratings)
   - Benefits over features

3. **Simplify**
   - Remove distractions
   - Shorter content
   - Mobile-optimized

### Low Conversion Rates?

**Symptoms:**
- Opens and clicks are good, but no purchases
- Conversion rate < 10%

**Solutions:**
1. **Offer/incentive**
   - Increase discount (10% â†’ 15%)
   - Add free gift
   - Free delivery
   - Limited time offer

2. **Remove friction**
   - Link directly to product (not homepage)
   - Pre-apply discount code
   - One-click checkout
   - Guest checkout option

3. **Build trust**
   - Customer reviews
   - Money-back guarantee
   - Secure payment badges
   - Contact information

### High Exit Rates?

**Symptoms:**
- Customers exit playbook before completing

**Solutions:**
1. **Shorten playbook**
   - 14 days â†’ 7 days
   - Reduce wait times between steps
   - Get to offer faster

2. **Better exit conditions**
   - Exit after purchase (don't keep sending)
   - Respect unsubscribes
   - Move to different playbook (retention)

3. **Increase engagement**
   - More personalization
   - Interactive content
   - Ask for feedback

## Best Practices

<Callout type="tip">
**Start simple:** First playbook should be 3-5 steps max. Learn what works, then expand.

**Test everything:** A/B test subject lines, send times, offers. Data beats assumptions.

**Personalize:** Use customer name, past purchases, browsing history. Generic = ignored.

**Respect preferences:** Honor unsubscribes, Do Not Disturb, frequency caps. Trust > sales.

**Monitor closely:** First 2 weeks are critical. Watch data daily, optimize quickly.
</Callout>
