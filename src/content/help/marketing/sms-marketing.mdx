## Setting Up SMS Marketing

### Step 1: Connect Blackleaf

1. Go to **Settings ‚Üí Integrations ‚Üí Blackleaf**
2. Click **"Connect Account"**
3. Enter your Blackleaf credentials
   - Account ID
   - API Key (from Blackleaf dashboard)
4. Test connection
5. Enable SMS campaigns

**Don't have Blackleaf?** Markitbot can create an account for you (contact support).

### SMS Best Practices

<Callout type="warning">
**Character Limit:** Standard SMS is 160 characters. Markitbot auto-splits longer messages, but aim for 160 to minimize cost.
</Callout>

**Anatomy of a High-Converting SMS:**

```
[Emoji] [Hook] [Offer]
[CTA + Link]

[Opt-out]
```

**Example:**
```
üî• [Hook] FLASH SALE!
20% off flower today. [Offer]

Shop now: bit.ly/f20 [CTA + Link]

Reply STOP to opt out [Required]
```

**Emoji Usage:**
- ‚úÖ 1-2 emojis max
- ‚úÖ Relevant to message (üî• for sale, üåø for flower)
- ‚ùå Don't overdo it (looks spammy)

**Link Shortening:**
- Always use short links (bit.ly, Markitbot auto-shortens)
- Trackable (see click rates)
- Trust signals (avoid sketchy shorteners)

**Timing:**
| Day | Best Times | Avoid |
|-----|------------|-------|
| Mon-Fri | 10am-2pm, 5pm-7pm | Before 9am, after 9pm |
| Sat-Sun | 11am-3pm | Before 10am, after 8pm |

**Frequency:**
- 4-6 messages per month (max)
- Space at least 3 days apart
- Exception: Event reminders (can send day-of)

## Measuring SMS Performance

**Dashboard ‚Üí Marketing ‚Üí SMS ‚Üí Analytics**

### Key Metrics

**Delivery Rate:**
- Target: >97%
- Low? Check for invalid numbers, carrier blocks

**Open Rate:**
- Target: >95% (SMS opens ‚âà reads)
- SMS doesn't track "opens" like email, but delivery ‚âà open

**Click-Through Rate (CTR):**
- Target: >25%
- Measures link clicks / delivered messages

**Conversion Rate:**
- Target: >5%
- Measures purchases / link clicks

**Opt-Out Rate:**
- Target: <3%
- High? Sending too frequently or irrelevant content

**ROAS (Return on Ad Spend):**
- Target: >10x
- Formula: Revenue / SMS Cost

## Troubleshooting

### Low Click Rates (<20%)

**Causes:**
- ‚ùå Unclear offer
- ‚ùå Link not working
- ‚ùå Not mobile-optimized
- ‚ùå Sent at bad time

**Fixes:**
- ‚úÖ Clear, compelling offer
- ‚úÖ Test link before sending
- ‚úÖ Ensure mobile-friendly landing page
- ‚úÖ Send during peak times (10am-2pm)

### High Opt-Out Rates (>5%)

**Causes:**
- ‚ùå Too frequent (>6/month)
- ‚ùå Irrelevant content
- ‚ùå All sales, no value

**Fixes:**
- ‚úÖ Reduce frequency to 4/month
- ‚úÖ Segment by interest
- ‚úÖ Mix sales with tips, education (80/20 rule)

### Low Conversion Rates (<3%)

**Causes:**
- ‚ùå Offer not compelling
- ‚ùå Landing page issues
- ‚ùå Targeting wrong audience

**Fixes:**
- ‚úÖ Test different offers (10% vs. 20% vs. BOGO)
- ‚úÖ Optimize landing page for mobile
- ‚úÖ Segment campaigns by customer value
