## Generating Your First Vibe

### Method 1: Start from Preset

1. **Choose your vibe type** (Web or Mobile)
2. **Browse preset cards** - Click any preset to see preview
3. **Generate from preset** - Click "Generate" on preset card
4. **AI customizes** - Claude adapts preset to your prompt

**Example:**
- Preset: "Cyberpunk"
- Your input: "Make it more purple, less neon"
- Result: Cyberpunk aesthetic with purple accents, toned-down neon

### Method 2: Describe from Scratch

1. **Choose your vibe type** (Web or Mobile)
2. **Click "Custom Vibe"**
3. **Describe your vision** in natural language
4. **AI generates** - Claude creates theme from scratch

**Good prompts:**
```
"Earthy tones with lots of green, organic vibes,
think farmers market meets dispensary"

"Luxury cannabis experience, gold accents, dark
background, premium feel like a high-end hotel"

"Fun and playful, bright colors, friendly and
approachable for first-time cannabis buyers"
```

**Bad prompts:**
```
"Make it look good" (too vague)
"Blue" (not enough context)
"Like Apple" (too generic)
```

<Callout type="tip">
**Best prompt structure:**
1. Overall mood/aesthetic
2. Color preferences
3. Target audience or brand comparison
4. Any specific elements you want
</Callout>

## Best Practices

### Creating Effective Vibes

**Do:**
- ‚úÖ Start with a preset, then customize
- ‚úÖ Use descriptive prompts with context
- ‚úÖ Think about your target customer
- ‚úÖ Consider your brand colors/identity
- ‚úÖ Test both light and dark variations

**Don't:**
- ‚ùå Use vague prompts ("make it cool")
- ‚ùå Copy competitors exactly
- ‚ùå Ignore accessibility (contrast, readability)
- ‚ùå Overuse refinements (know when to start fresh)
- ‚ùå Forget mobile experience (test responsive)

### Brand Consistency

If you're a brand/dispensary, maintain consistency:

**Colors:**
- Use your existing brand colors as starting point
- Maintain 4.5:1 contrast ratio (WCAG AA)
- Test colors with actual product photos

**Typography:**
- Match your brand's font style (modern, classic, playful)
- Ensure readability at all sizes
- Consider mobile font sizes (16px+ for body)

**Voice:**
- Prompts should reflect your brand personality
- Formal? Casual? Playful? Professional?
