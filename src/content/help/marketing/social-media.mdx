## Platform-Specific Strategies

### Instagram

**Best for:** Visual storytelling, brand building, product showcasing

**Content types:**
- **Feed posts**: Product photos, lifestyle shots, education
- **Stories**: Behind-the-scenes, polls, Q&A, daily updates
- **Reels**: Trending content, how-tos, product demos
- **IGTV**: Long-form education, interviews, events

**Posting frequency:**
- Feed: 3-5x per week
- Stories: Daily
- Reels: 2-3x per week

**Best practices:**
- Use high-quality product photography
- Include educational captions (effects, terpenes, usage)
- Engage with comments within 1 hour
- Use relevant hashtags (15-20 per post)
- Tag location for local discovery

### Facebook

**Best for:** Community building, events, longer-form content

**Content types:**
- **Posts**: News, articles, education, events
- **Groups**: Build private community
- **Events**: Promote launches, vendor days
- **Live**: Q&A sessions, product demos

**Posting frequency:** 3-5x per week

**Best practices:**
- Create Facebook Group for community
- Share educational content (articles, guides)
- Promote events (local reach)
- Respond to all comments/messages
- Follow platform policies strictly

### Twitter/X

**Best for:** Industry news, quick updates, conversations

**Content types:**
- Product drops
- Industry news/advocacy
- Quick tips
- Engage in cannabis community discussions

**Posting frequency:** 1-3x per day

**Best practices:**
- Use trending hashtags (#Cannabis, #CannabisNews)
- Engage with cannabis influencers
- Share industry articles
- Participate in #CannabisTwitter conversations

### TikTok

**Best for:** Younger audience (21-30), viral content, education

**Content types:**
- Educational shorts (strain types, terpenes)
- "Day in the life" (dispensary, budtender)
- Product unboxing
- Cannabis myths debunked

**Posting frequency:** 3-5x per week

**Best practices:**
- Use trending sounds/music
- Keep videos under 60 seconds
- Educational + entertaining
- Avoid showing consumption on camera
- Clear 21+ disclaimers

## Content Pillars

### 1. Educational Content (40%)

**Teach your audience:**
- Strain types (Indica, Sativa, Hybrid)
- Terpene profiles and effects
- Consumption methods
- Dosing guidelines
- Cannabis science

**Example posts:**
- "Terpene Tuesday: What is Myrcene?"
- "Indica vs Sativa: The Real Difference"
- "How to Choose the Right Edible Dose"

### 2. Product Highlights (30%)

**Showcase your products:**
- New arrivals
- Best sellers
- Featured strains
- Product close-ups

**Example posts:**
- "New Drop: Purple Punch"
- "Staff Pick of the Week"
- "Behind the Jar: Blue Dream"

### 3. Brand Storytelling (20%)

**Build emotional connection:**
- Your origin story
- Meet the team
- Behind-the-scenes
- Company values

**Example posts:**
- "Meet Sarah, our lead budtender"
- "How we source our products"
- "Our commitment to sustainability"

### 4. Community Engagement (10%)

**Connect with audience:**
- User-generated content (repost customer photos)
- Polls and questions
- Contests (where legal)
- Local community involvement

**Example posts:**
- "Which strain should we stock next? Vote below"
- "Shout out to @customer for this awesome photo!"
- "We donated to [local charity] this month"

## Growing Your Following

### Organic Growth Tactics

**1. Consistent posting:**
- Post 3-5x per week minimum
- Same days/times for consistency
- Quality over quantity

**2. Engagement:**
- Respond to all comments
- Like and comment on others' posts
- Follow relevant accounts
- DM new followers (thank you message)

**3. Collaborations:**
- Partner with local cannabis brands
- Cross-promote with complementary businesses
- Guest takeovers
- Shoutout-for-shoutout

**4. Local targeting:**
- Use location tags
- Engage with local cannabis community
- Comment on local news/events
- Partner with local influencers

### Influencer Partnerships

**Work with cannabis influencers:**

**Micro-influencers (1k-10k followers):**
- More affordable ($100-500 per post)
- Higher engagement rates
- Local reach
- Authentic audience

**Strategy:**
1. Identify local cannabis influencers
2. Offer free products for review
3. Request honest post/story
4. Track results (use unique discount code)

**Example collab:**
```
Influencer posts:
"Just tried @YourBrand's Blue Dream and wow!
Perfect daytime strain. Link in my bio for 15% off
with code: INFLUENCER15"

Result: 42 orders, $2,940 revenue, 187 new followers
```

## Measuring Success

**Key metrics to track:**

**Engagement:**
- Likes per post (target: 3-5% of followers)
- Comments per post (target: 0.5-1% of followers)
- Shares/saves (indicates valuable content)
- Engagement rate: (Likes + Comments) / Followers

**Growth:**
- Follower growth rate (target: 5-10% per month)
- Profile visits
- Link clicks (bio link)

**Conversion:**
- Traffic to website (from bio link)
- Discount code usage
- New customers from social

**Tools:**
- Instagram Insights (built-in analytics)
- Facebook Business Suite
- Third-party: Later, Buffer, Hootsuite
