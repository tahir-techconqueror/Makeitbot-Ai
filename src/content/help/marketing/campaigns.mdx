# Creating Marketing Campaigns

Launch SMS, email, and social media campaigns with Drip (Marketing Automation Agent). From planning to execution in minutes, not hours.

## Campaign Types

Markitbot supports three campaign channels:

| Channel | Best For | Engagement Rate | Cost |
|---------|----------|-----------------|------|
| **SMS** | Urgent offers, flash sales, limited-time deals | 98% open rate, 36% click rate | $0.01-0.03/msg |
| **Email** | Product launches, newsletters, educational content | 20% open rate, 3% click rate | $0.001/email |
| **Social** | Brand awareness, community building, visual content | Varies by platform | Free (organic) |

<Callout type="tip">
**Multi-channel works best**: Send SMS for urgent promotions, email for details, social for brand building. Drip can coordinate across all three.
</Callout>

## Creating Your First Campaign

### Step 1: Navigate to Campaigns

1. Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí Marketing ‚Üí Campaigns**
2. Click **"Create Campaign"**
3. Choose campaign type:
   - SMS (via Blackleaf)
   - Email (via Mailjet)

   - Multi-channel (combine SMS + Email + Social)

### Step 2: Define Campaign Goals

Drip asks for:

**Campaign Name**: Internal reference (e.g., "420 Sale 2026")

**Goal**: What do you want to achieve?
- Drive sales (product promotion)
- Brand awareness (new product launch)

**Target Audience**:
- All customers
- New customers (first purchase within 30 days)
- VIP customers (high lifetime value)
- Lapsed customers (no purchase in 60+ days)
- Custom segment (by location, product preference, etc.)

**Timeline**:
- Send immediately
- Schedule for specific date/time
- Recurring (weekly deals, monthly newsletter)

## Campaign Templates

Drip provides pre-built templates for common campaigns:

### 1. Flash Sale
**When to use**: Limited-time discount (24-48 hours)

**Template includes:**
- Urgency language ("Today only!", "Limited time!")
- Clear discount (20% off, BOGO, etc.)
- CTA (Shop now, Use code, Visit store)
- Expiration date

### 2. New Product Launch
**When to use**: Introducing new strain, product line, or category

**Template includes:**
- Product hero image
- Key features/benefits
- Strain info (Indica/Sativa, effects)
- Early access or launch discount
- Educational content (how to use, what to expect)

### 3. Customer Win-Back
**When to use**: Re-engage lapsed customers (60+ days no purchase)

**Template includes:**
- Personalized greeting
- "We miss you" messaging
- Incentive to return (15% off, loyalty points)
- Showcase new products since last visit

### 4. Loyalty Rewards
**When to use**: Thank VIP customers, reward repeat purchases

**Template includes:**
- Recognition ("You're a VIP!")
- Exclusive offer (early access, extra discount)
- Loyalty points balance
- Referral program mention

### 5. Educational Content
**When to use**: Build trust, position as expert

**Template includes:**
- Cannabis education (strain types, consumption methods)
- Product guides (how to choose edibles)
- Industry news
- Minimal sales pitch (soft CTA at end)

### 6. Event Promotion
**When to use**: Dispensary opening, vendor day, 420 party

**Template includes:**
- Event details (date, time, location)
- What to expect (deals, samples, activities)
- RSVP or ticket link
- Parking/directions

## Campaign Best Practices

### SMS Best Practices

<Callout type="warning">
**SMS Compliance Rules:**
- Always include opt-out ("Reply STOP")
- Send only to opted-in contacts
- Avoid sending after 9pm local time
- Limit frequency (max 4-6/month recommended)
- Include brand name
</Callout>

**Timing:**
- **Best times**: Tues-Thurs, 10am-2pm or 5pm-7pm
- **Worst times**: Early morning (<9am), late night (>9pm), Mondays

**Content:**
- Keep under 160 characters
- Front-load key info (discount, deadline)
- Use emojis sparingly (1-2 max)
- Include clear CTA with link

### Email Best Practices

**Subject Lines:**
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use emojis to stand out üåø
- Create urgency ("Last Chance", "Ending Soon")
- Personalize ([FirstName], "Your favorites are back")
- A/B test different approaches

**Content:**
- Mobile-first design (60% open on mobile)
- Hero image at top (product or offer)
- Clear single CTA (don't overwhelm with options)
- Personalize with name, past purchases
- Footer: Unsubscribe link, contact info, social links

**Timing:**
- **Best days**: Tues, Wed, Thurs
- **Best times**: 10am (morning coffee), 2pm (post-lunch), 6pm (after work)
- **Worst days**: Weekends (lower open rates)

### Social Media Best Practices

**General Tips:**
- Use high-quality visuals
- Include 5-10 relevant hashtags
- Tag location (for local discovery)
- Post consistently (3-5x/week)
- Engage with comments within 1 hour

**Platform-Specific:**

**Instagram:**
- Image/Carousel posts for products
- Stories for behind-the-scenes, polls
- Reels for trending content
- Hashtags: #Cannabis #[City]Cannabis #Indica #Sativa

**Facebook:**
- Longer-form content (articles, videos)
- Community building (groups, events)
- Facebook Ads (if compliant with policies)

**Twitter/X:**
- Industry news, quick updates
- Engage in cannabis community conversations
- Use relevant hashtags #Cannabis #Weed

## Advanced Features

### A/B Testing

Test variations to improve performance:

**What to test:**
- Subject lines (short vs. long)
- Discount amount (15% vs. 20% vs. BOGO)
- CTA text ("Shop Now" vs. "Get Yours" vs. "Claim Offer")
- Send time (morning vs. afternoon)

**How it works:**
1. Create two versions
2. Send each to 50% of audience
3. Drip tracks which performs better
4. Use winner for future campaigns

### Personalization

Drip personalizes campaigns with:
- Customer name
- Past purchases ("We noticed you love Sativas...")
- Location ("Available at [NearbyDispensary]")
- Loyalty status ("VIP Member Exclusive")

### Segmentation

Target specific customer groups:

**By Purchase Behavior:**
- High-value customers (>$500 lifetime)
- Flower buyers only
- Edibles buyers only
- First-time buyers (within 30 days)

**By Engagement:**
- Email openers (engaged)
- Email non-openers (need re-engagement)
- SMS clickers (highly engaged)

**By Demographics:**
- Location (city, state)
- Age group (21-30, 31-45, 46+)
- Medical vs. adult-use

## Troubleshooting

### Low Open Rates

**SMS (target 90%+):**
- ‚ùå Poor: 70%
- ‚úÖ Check: List quality (remove old numbers)
- ‚úÖ Test: Send times (avoid late night)

**Email (target 20%+):**
- ‚ùå Poor: 10%
- ‚úÖ Fix: Improve subject lines (A/B test)
- ‚úÖ Clean: Remove inactive subscribers

### Low Click Rates

**SMS (target 25%+):**
- ‚ùå Poor: 10%
- ‚úÖ Fix: Stronger CTA, clearer offer
- ‚úÖ Check: Link works on mobile

**Email (target 3%+):**
- ‚ùå Poor: <1%
- ‚úÖ Fix: Larger CTA buttons
- ‚úÖ Simplify: Too many links dilutes focus

### Low Conversions

**Target: 2-5%**
- ‚ùå Poor: <1%
- ‚úÖ Fix: Landing page optimization
- ‚úÖ Check: Discount code works
- ‚úÖ Test: Higher discount (15% ‚Üí 20%)
