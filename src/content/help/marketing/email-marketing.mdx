## Email Design Best Practices

### Template Structure

**Recommended Layout:**
```

[Headline (H1)]

[Body Copy (2-4 paragraphs)]

[Product Grid or CTA Button]

```

### Mobile Optimization

**Critical:** 60%+ of emails opened on mobile

âœ… **Do:**
- Single column layout
- Large, tappable buttons (44px min)
- Text: 14px+ font size
- Subject line: <50 characters
- Pre-header text visible

âŒ **Don't:**
- Multi-column layouts (hard to read on mobile)
- Tiny text or buttons
- Image-only emails (may not load)

### Subject Line Best Practices

**Length:** 40-50 characters (shorter on mobile)

**Tactics:**
- **Urgency:** "Ends Tonight", "Last Chance", "24 Hours Only"
- **Personalization:** "Hi [FirstName]", "Your Favorites Are Back"
- **Curiosity:** "You Won't Believe This New Strain", "The Secret to Better Sleep"
- **Value:** "Save 25%", "Free Gift Inside", "Exclusive Access"
- **Emojis:** ðŸ”¥ ðŸŒ¿ ðŸ’š (1-2 max, relevant)

**A/B Test:**
- Test 2 subject lines on 20% of audience

## Measuring Email Performance

**Dashboard â†’ Marketing â†’ Email â†’ Analytics**

### Key Metrics

**Delivery Rate:**
- Target: >97%
- Low? Clean your list (remove bounces)

**Open Rate:**
- Target: >20% (cannabis avg)
- Top performers: >30%
- Low? Test subject lines, send times

**Click-Through Rate (CTR):**
- Target: >3%
- Top performers: >5%
- Low? Improve CTA, design, content relevance

**Conversion Rate:**
- Target: >2%
- Top performers: >5%
- Low? Optimize landing page, offer

**ROAS (Return on Ad Spend):**
- Target: >15x
- Formula: Revenue / Email Cost

**Unsubscribe Rate:**
- Target: <0.5%
- High (>2%)? Sending too frequently or irrelevant

### Content Best Practices

âœ… **Balance text/images** (not image-only emails)
âœ… **Include unsubscribe** (legally required, improves deliverability)

### List Hygiene
- Remove hard bounces immediately
- Remove soft bounces after 3 attempts
- Remove unengaged subscribers (no opens in 6 months)
- Re-engagement campaign before removing
