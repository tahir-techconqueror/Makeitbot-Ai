## Key Metrics Explained

### Revenue Metrics

**Total Revenue:**
- All sales (completed orders only)
- Includes tax (if displaying OTD pricing)
- Excludes refunds and cancellations

**Revenue Growth:**
- Comparison to previous period
- +12% = growing, -8% = declining
- Green = positive, red = negative

**Average Order Value (AOV):**
- Total revenue / number of orders
- Goal: Increase over time (upselling, bundles)
- Industry avg: $45-65 for cannabis

**Revenue by Category:**
```
Flower: $1,225 (50%)
Vapes: $735 (30%)
Edibles: $367 (15%)
Other: $123 (5%)
```
- Shows product mix
- Identify top performers
- Spot declining categories

### Customer Metrics

**Total Customers:**
- All customers in database
- Active + inactive

**New Customers:**
- First purchase today/this period
- Measure acquisition efforts

**Returning Customers:**
- 2+ purchases
- Measure retention and loyalty
- Goal: 60%+ returning rate

**Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):**
- Avg revenue per customer over lifetime
- Formula: AOV Ã— Purchase Frequency Ã— Lifespan
- Cannabis avg: $800-1,500

**Retention Rate:**
- % of customers who return
- 30-day, 60-day, 90-day rates
- Goal: 60%+ at 90 days

### Order Metrics

**Total Orders:**
- All completed orders
- Excludes cancelled

**Orders by Type:**
```
Pickup: 29 (62%)
Delivery: 18 (38%)
```
- Pickup typically higher volume, lower AOV
- Delivery typically lower volume, higher AOV

**Orders by Status:**
```
New: 3 (need attention!)
Preparing: 5
Ready: 2
Completed: 37
```
- "New" orders need confirmation
- Long "Preparing" times = staffing issue

**On-Time Fulfillment:**
- % of orders completed on time
- Goal: 95%+
- Impacts customer satisfaction

### Marketing Metrics

**Active Campaigns:**
- Currently running campaigns
- SMS, email, social

**Email Open Rate:**
- % of recipients who opened
- Industry avg: 20-25%
- Goal: 25%+

**SMS Click Rate:**
- % who clicked link
- Industry avg: 30-40%
- Goal: 35%+

**Low Stock Alerts:**

- Restock before running out

**Out of Stock:**
- Products with 0 inventory
- Impacts sales (can't sell what you don't have)
- Goal: <5% of catalog

**Inventory Turnover:**
- How fast products sell
- Formula: COGS / Avg Inventory Value
- Goal: 8-12x per year (cannabis)

## Using Dashboard Data

### Daily Check-In (5 minutes)

**Every morning:**
1. Check revenue vs. goal
2. Review new orders (confirm any pending)
3. Check low stock alerts (restock)
4. Review top products (ensure in stock)

### Weekly Review (30 minutes)

**Every Monday:**
1. Revenue trend (up, down, flat?)
2. Top products (changed from last week?)
3. Marketing performance (which campaigns worked?)
4. Customer metrics (retention improving?)
5. Plan next week's actions

### Monthly Analysis (2 hours)

**First of each month:**
1. Compare to goals
2. Category analysis (which growing/declining?)
3. Customer cohort analysis
4. Campaign ROI review
5. Inventory optimization
6. Set next month's goals

## Common Dashboard Questions

**Q: Why is today's revenue lower than yesterday?**
A: Could be day-of-week effect (Mondays slower), weather, or random variance. Check weekly/monthly trend instead.

**Q: AOV dropping - is that bad?**
A: Not always. Could mean more small orders (good for volume). Check total revenue trend.

**Q: When should I worry about metrics?**
A: 7-day trends more reliable than single days. 3+ weeks of decline = investigate.

**Q: How do I set realistic goals?**
A: Start with last month's numbers, add 10-15% growth. Adjust based on results.
