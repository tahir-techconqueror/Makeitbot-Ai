## Using Customer Insights

### Personalized Marketing

**Example: VIP customer campaign**

```
Segment: Champions (152 customers)
Message: "You're a VIP! Exclusive early access..."
Channel: Email + SMS
Discount: 20% off new products
Result: 78% open rate, 42% conversion, $18,240 revenue
```

**Example: Win-back campaign**

```
Segment: At-Risk (187 customers)
Message: "We miss you! Here's 25% off..."
Channel: Email
Discount: 25% off next order
Result: 28% win-back rate, $12,680 recovered revenue
```
