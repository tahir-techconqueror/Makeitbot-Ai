## What is a Sales Funnel?

A sales funnel tracks the customer journey through stages:

```

### Cannabis-Specific Funnel Stages

**1. Awareness â†’ Interest**
- **Website visit** (landing page, homepage)
- **Browse menu** (category pages)
- **Product discovery** (search, filters)

**Drop-off causes:**
- Poor first impression
- Slow site speed
- Unclear navigation
- No appealing products

**2. Interest â†’ Consideration**
- **Product page views** (detailed product info)
- **Ember chat** (ask questions)
- **Compare products** (vs. alternatives)

**Drop-off causes:**
- Insufficient product info
- No images or poor quality
- Out of stock
- Price too high

**3. Consideration â†’ Intent**
- **Add to cart** (decision made)
- **View cart** (review selection)
- **Apply discount** (if available)

**Drop-off causes:**
- Cart abandonment
- High shipping costs
- No delivery option
- Second thoughts

**4. Intent â†’ Purchase**
- **Start checkout** (enter info)
- **Payment** (submit order)
- **Order confirmation** (success)

**Drop-off causes:**
- Complicated checkout
- Payment issues
- Delivery concerns
- Age verification friction

## Improving Funnel Performance

### Stage 1: Awareness â†’ Interest (+10%)

**Goal:** Get more visitors to view products

**Tactics:**

**1. Homepage Optimization**
```
Before:
Generic "Welcome" message
Static product grid
No clear CTA

After:
Personalized greeting (based on location/time)
Featured products with photos
Clear CTA: "Shop Our Bestsellers"

Result: +15% product page visits
```

```
