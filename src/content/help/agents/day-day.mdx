## Key Features

### Keyword Research & Tracking
- **Discovers** high-value keywords for your market
- **Tracks rankings** for your target keywords
- **Monitors competitors** ranking for same terms
- **Identifies opportunities** (low competition, high volume)

### On-Page SEO Optimization
- **Meta tags** (title, description) optimization
- **Header structure** (H1, H2, H3) for clarity
- **Image optimization** (alt text, compression)
- **Internal linking** (connect related pages)
- **Schema markup** (structured data for rich results)

### Technical SEO
- **Site speed** monitoring and optimization
- **Mobile-friendliness** checks
- **Crawl error** detection and fixes
- **Sitemap** generation and submission
- **SSL/HTTPS** verification

### Content Strategy
- **Content gaps** (topics you should cover)
- **Content optimization** (improve existing pages)
- **Blog post ideas** based on search trends
- **FAQ schema** (appear in Google's "People also ask")

### Local SEO (Critical for Cannabis)
- **Google Business Profile** optimization
- **Local citations** (directories, listings)
- **NAP consistency** (Name, Address, Phone)
- **Review generation** (get more Google reviews)
- **Local pack rankings** ("cannabis near me")

## SEO Best Practices

<Callout type="tip">
**Patience is key**: SEO takes 3-6 months to show results. Keep creating content, optimizing pages, and building authority.

**Quality over quantity**: One great 1,500-word post beats ten thin 200-word posts.

**Mobile-first**: 70% of cannabis searches happen on mobile. Ensure your site works perfectly on phones.

**Local is critical**: "Cannabis near me" is the #1 search. Optimize for local pack rankings.
</Callout>
