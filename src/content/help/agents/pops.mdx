# Pulse - Data Analyst

Your AI data analyst who turns raw data into actionable insights, forecasts trends, and measures what matters for your cannabis business.

## What Pulse Does

**Pulse is your data expert** who:
- Analyzes sales, customer, and marketing data
- Creates visual reports and dashboards
- Forecasts revenue and demand
- Identifies trends and anomalies
- Measures campaign ROI
- Answers "why" questions about your business

<Callout type="info">
**Why data matters:** Gut feeling is important, but data reveals what's actually working. Pulse helps you make decisions based on facts, not assumptions.
</Callout>

## Key Features

### Sales Analytics
- **Revenue tracking** (daily, weekly, monthly)
- **Product performance** (bestsellers, slow movers)
- **Category analysis** (flower vs. vapes vs. edibles)
- **Margin analysis** (gross profit by product)
- **Growth rates** (MoM, YoY comparisons)

### Customer Analytics
- **Cohort analysis** (customer behavior by signup month)
- **Lifetime value (LTV)** calculations
- **Acquisition costs** (CAC by channel)
- **Retention curves** (how many customers stay)
- **Purchase patterns** (frequency, basket size)

### Marketing Analytics
- **Campaign ROI** (revenue per campaign)
- **Channel performance** (SMS vs. email vs. social)
- **Conversion funnels** (where customers drop off)
- **Attribution** (which touchpoints drove sales)

### Forecasting
- **Revenue predictions** (next 30/60/90 days)
- **Demand forecasting** (which products to stock)
- **Seasonal trends** (420, holidays, summer)
- **Scenario modeling** ("what if we raised prices 10%?")

## Key Metrics Pulse Tracks

### Financial Metrics
- **Revenue** (total sales)
- **Gross Profit** (revenue - COGS)
- **Gross Margin** (gross profit / revenue)
- **Average Order Value (AOV)** (revenue / orders)
- **Revenue Per Customer** (revenue / unique customers)

### Customer Metrics
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** (marketing spend / new customers)
- **Lifetime Value (LTV)** (avg revenue per customer over lifetime)
- **LTV:CAC Ratio** (should be 3:1 or higher)
- **Retention Rate** (% customers who return)
- **Churn Rate** (% customers who leave)

### Product Metrics
- **Sell-Through Rate** (units sold / units stocked)
- **Inventory Turnover** (COGS / avg inventory)
- **Days to Sell** (365 / inventory turnover)
- **Product Margin** (profit per product)

### Marketing Metrics
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)** (revenue / ad spend)
- **Conversion Rate** (purchases / visitors)
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** (clicks / impressions)
- **Engagement Rate** (interactions / reach)

## Best Practices

<Callout type="tip">
**Check dashboards weekly**: Make it a habit. Every Monday, review key metrics with Pulse.

**Ask "why" questions**: Don't just see revenue dropped, ask Pulse why. Root cause analysis drives action.

**Track trends, not just snapshots**: One bad week isn't a pattern. Look at 30-60-90 day trends.

**Compare apples to apples**: Last week vs. same week last year (accounts for seasonality).
</Callout>
