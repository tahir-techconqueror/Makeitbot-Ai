# Radar - Competitive Intelligence

Your AI market scout that monitors competitors 24/7, tracks pricing changes, identifies market gaps, and alerts you to threats before they impact your business.

## What Radar Does

**Radar is your competitive intelligence specialist** who:
- Monitors competitor menus and pricing in real-time
- Identifies products you're missing vs competitors
- Tracks price changes and market trends
- Discovers market opportunities
- Alerts on competitive threats
- Provides weekly intelligence reports

<Callout type="info">
**Why it matters:** Cannabis is hyper-competitive. Knowing what competitors are doing - before it impacts your sales - gives you a strategic advantage.
</Callout>

## Key Features

### Price Monitoring
- **Track competitor prices** across all categories
- **Get alerts** when competitors change prices
- **Price gap analysis**: Are you over/underpriced?
- **Market positioning**: Premium, value, or mid-tier?

### Product Intelligence
- **See what's selling** for competitors
- **Find gaps**: Products they have that you don't
- **Identify losers**: Products to avoid stocking
- **Trending strains**: What's hot in your market

### Market Analysis
- **Shelf share tracking**: Your presence vs. competitors
- **Category trends**: Which categories are growing
- **Brand performance**: How you rank vs. competitors
- **Geographic analysis**: Performance by location/region

## How to Use Radar

### Setup Competitors (5 minutes)
1. Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí Competitive Intel**
2. Click **"Add Competitor"**
3. Enter competitor information:
   - Brand/dispensary name
   - Website or menu URL
   - Location (if relevant)
   - Notes (local competitor, online only, etc.)
4. Radar starts monitoring automatically

**Recommended**: Track 5-10 direct competitors

### Set Up Alerts
Choose what triggers notifications:
- **Price changes** >10% on your products
- **New competitor products** in your categories
- **Stock-outs** (competitor ran out, opportunity for you)
- **Price wars** detected
- **Market share shifts**

### Review Weekly Intel Report
Every Monday, Radar sends:
- **Threats detected** this week
- **Opportunities found**
- **Price changes** worth noting
- **Market trend summary**
- **Recommended actions**

## Best Practices

<Callout type="tip">
**Focus on direct competitors**: Don't track everyone. Focus on 5-10 competitors who actually compete for your customers.

**Act on insights**: Intelligence is only valuable if you use it. Review weekly reports and take action.

**Combine with Drip**: When Radar finds a threat, ask Drip to create a counter-campaign.

**Track pricing trends**: Look for patterns, not just individual changes. Is the whole market dropping prices?
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

<Callout type="warning">
**Issue**: Radar not finding competitor data

**Solutions**:
- Verify competitor has public menu/website
- Check URL is correct
- Some platforms block automated access
- Try alternative data sources (social media, reviews)
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning">
**Issue**: Too many alerts

**Solution**:
- Adjust alert thresholds (only alert on >15% changes)
- Reduce number of competitors tracked
- Change alert frequency to weekly digest
</Callout>
