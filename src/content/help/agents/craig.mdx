# Drip - Marketing Automation

Your AI marketing automation specialist that creates campaigns, writes compliance-checked copy, and manages SMS/email outreach - all while keeping your brand voice consistent.

## What Drip Does

**Drip is your marketing automation specialist** who handles:
- Campaign creation (SMS, Email, Social media)
- Marketing copy generation (Professional, Hype, Educational tones)
- Compliance checking (with Sentinel before sending)
- Customer segmentation
- Performance tracking
- Automated playbooks

<Callout type="success">
**The key benefit:** Drip creates marketing content 10x faster than humans, with built-in compliance checking, and learns your brand voice over time.
</Callout>

## Campaign Types

### SMS Campaigns (via Blackleaf)
- **Promotional messages**: Sales, new products, events
- **Loyalty updates**: Points earned, VIP status, rewards
- **Abandoned cart reminders**
- **Restocked notifications**

**Character limit**: 160 characters (Drip optimizes for this)

### Email Campaigns (via Mailjet)
- **Product launches**: Full descriptions with images
- **Educational content**: Strain guides, usage tips
- **Monthly newsletters**: What's new, bestsellers, stories
- **Personalized recommendations**: Based on purchase history

**Best for**: Longer-form content, visual storytelling

### Social Media Posts
- **Instagram**: Product photos with captions
- **TikTok**: Short video scripts
- **Twitter/X**: Quick updates and engagement
- **Facebook**: Community posts and events
- **LinkedIn**: B2B content for retailers

## How to Create a Campaign

### Quick Campaign (5 minutes)
1. Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí Marketing ‚Üí New Campaign**
2. Choose campaign type (SMS, Email, Social)
3. Tell Drip what you want:
   - "Announce 4/20 sale, 25% off flower"
   - "Promote new Blue Dream strain"
   - "Send abandoned cart reminders"
4. Drip generates 3 content variations
5. Review and select your favorite
6. Sentinel checks compliance automatically
7. Schedule or send immediately

### Advanced Campaign Wizard
1. **Set Goal**
   - Drive sales
   - Launch new product
   - Build awareness
   - Retain customers
   - Event promotion

2. **Choose Audience**
   - All customers
   - VIP members only
   - Recent buyers
   - Lapsed customers (haven't bought in 30+ days)
   - By location or demographics

3. **Generate Content**
   - Drip creates copy in your brand voice
   - Suggests images from your product catalog
   - Adds personalization tokens ({{firstName}}, {{favoriteProduct}})

4. **Compliance Check**
   - Sentinel reviews for violations
   - Flags medical claims, age requirements, prohibited language
   - Suggests compliant alternatives

5. **Schedule & Send**
   - Send now or schedule for later
   - Optimal send times suggested by Drip
   - A/B test options available

## Marketing Playbooks

**Playbooks = Automated marketing workflows**

### Pre-Built Playbooks

**New Product Launch**
1. Week before: Teaser email
2. Launch day: SMS blast
3. Day 2: Instagram post
4. Week after: Follow-up with buyers

**Abandoned Cart Recovery**
1. 1 hour: Reminder SMS
2. 24 hours: Email with product photos
3. 48 hours: Limited-time discount offer

**Re-engagement Campaign**
1. Identify customers who haven't bought in 60 days
2. Personalized "we miss you" email
3. Special comeback offer
4. SMS reminder if no response

**VIP Nurture**
1. Monthly exclusive offers
2. Early access to new products
3. Birthday rewards
4. Loyalty milestone celebrations

### Custom Playbooks
Create your own automated workflows:
1. Define trigger (new signup, purchase, inactivity)
2. Set timing (immediate, delay, recurring)
3. Choose channel (SMS, Email, both)
4. Drip generates content
5. Activate and monitor

## Integrations

### SMS Platform: Blackleaf
- **Setup**: Enter API key in Settings ‚Üí Integrations
- **Features**: Two-way messaging, opt-in management, delivery tracking
- **Cost**: ~$0.01 per message (you pay Blackleaf directly)
- **Compliance**: TCPA-compliant opt-in/out handling

### Email Platform: Mailjet
- **Setup**: Connect via OAuth in Settings ‚Üí Integrations
- **Features**: Templates, analytics, deliverability tracking
- **Cost**: Free tier up to 6,000 emails/month
- **Design**: Drag-and-drop templates or HTML

### Social Media
- **Instagram**: Post directly or copy to clipboard
- **TikTok**: Generate video scripts
- **Twitter/Facebook**: One-click posting
- **LinkedIn**: B2B-focused content

## Best Practices

<Callout type="tip">
**Keep your customer list updated**: Drip's recommendations are only as good as your customer data. Sync your POS or manually update customer preferences.

**Test send times**: Drip suggests optimal times but A/B testing helps find what works for YOUR audience.

**Segment wisely**: Targeted campaigns perform 5-10x better than blasts to everyone.

**Let Sentinel review**: ALWAYS let compliance checking run before sending to avoid violations.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

<Callout type="warning">
**Issue**: Low email open rates

**Solutions**:
- Test different subject lines (Drip generates variations)
- Send at different times (morning vs evening)
- Clean your email list (remove bounces)
- Check spam score (Mailjet provides this)
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning">
**Issue**: High unsubscribe rates

**Solutions**:
- Reduce email frequency
- Better segment your audience
- Improve content relevance
- Add value (not just promotions)
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning">
**Issue**: SMS compliance violations

**Solution**:
- Always use Sentinel's compliance check
- Verify opt-in records
- Include opt-out instructions
- Follow TCPA guidelines
</Callout>
