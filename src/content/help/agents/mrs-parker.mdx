# Mrs. Parker - Customer Retention

Your AI customer retention specialist who predicts churn, designs loyalty programs, and personalizes outreach to keep customers coming back.

## What Mrs. Parker Does

**Mrs. Parker is your retention expert** who:
- Identifies at-risk customers before they churn
- Designs personalized win-back campaigns
- Optimizes loyalty programs and rewards
- Segments customers by behavior and value
- Predicts lifetime value (LTV)
- Automates re-engagement workflows

<Callout type="success">
**Why retention matters:** Acquiring a new customer costs 5-7x more than retaining an existing one. A 5% increase in retention can increase profits by 25-95%.
</Callout>

## Key Features

### Churn Prediction
- **Risk scoring** (0-100) for each customer
- **Early warning alerts** (customers likely to leave)
- **Churn reasons** (price, product availability, experience)
- **Intervention recommendations** (what to do to save them)

### Customer Segmentation
Automatically groups customers by:
- **VIPs**: High lifetime value (top 20%)
- **Loyalists**: Frequent buyers (3+ purchases)
- **At-Risk**: Declining engagement
- **Lost**: No purchase in 90+ days
- **New**: First purchase within 30 days

### Personalized Outreach
- **Win-back campaigns** for lapsed customers
- **VIP rewards** for top spenders
- **Birthday/anniversary messages**
- **Product recommendations** based on history
- **Exclusive early access** to new products

### Loyalty Program Optimization
- **Points balance tracking**
- **Tier management** (Bronze, Silver, Gold)
- **Reward suggestions** (what motivates each segment)
- **ROI analysis** (program cost vs. retention value)

## Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

Mrs. Parker calculates LTV for each customer and segment:

**LTV Formula:**
```
Average Order Value √ó Purchase Frequency √ó Customer Lifespan

Example:
Sarah M.
- Avg Order: $45
- Frequency: 2.5x per month
- Lifespan: 18 months (so far)
```

## Common Retention Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Ignoring Churn Signals
**Problem**: Customer stops buying, you don't notice until it's too late

**Fix**: Set up Mrs. Parker's automatic alerts
- High-risk customers flagged immediately
- Automated win-back workflows
- Weekly retention report

### Mistake 2: Treating All Customers the Same
**Problem**: Same offer to VIPs and one-time buyers

**Fix**: Segment-based strategies
- VIPs: Personal attention, bigger incentives
- New customers: Education, relationship building
- At-risk: Targeted offers addressing their specific concern

### Mistake 3: Weak Loyalty Program
**Problem**: Generic points system, no differentiation

**Fix**: Mrs. Parker optimizes tiers
- Bronze: Basics
- Silver: Perks that matter (free delivery)
- Gold: Exclusive experiences (not just discounts)

### Mistake 4: No Win-Back Strategy
**Problem**: Customers leave, never hear from you again

**Fix**: Automated win-back sequences
- 60 days: First outreach
- 75 days: Stronger incentive
- 90 days: Final offer
- Personalized by segment

## Advanced Features

### RFM Analysis
Mrs. Parker uses Recency, Frequency, Monetary analysis:

**Recency:** How recently did they purchase?
- <30 days: Active
- 30-60 days: Warm
- 60-90 days: At-risk
- 90+ days: Lost

**Frequency:** How often do they buy?
- 4+ per month: Very high
- 2-3 per month: High
- 1 per month: Average
- <1 per month: Low

**Monetary:** How much do they spend?
- $100+/order: High value
- $50-99/order: Medium value
- <$50/order: Low value

**RFM Segments:**
- **Champions**: High on all three (VIPs)
- **Loyal**: High frequency, medium monetary
- **Big Spenders**: High monetary, low frequency
- **At-Risk**: High monetary, but declining recency/frequency
