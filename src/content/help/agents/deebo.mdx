# Sentinel - Compliance Officer

Your AI compliance enforcer that keeps your marketing, packaging, and content legal across all 50+ cannabis markets.

## What Sentinel Does

**Sentinel is your compliance guardian** who:
- Monitors 412+ state and local cannabis regulations
- Validates all marketing content before it goes live
- Checks product claims for legality
- Audits existing content for violations
- Alerts on new regulation changes
- Protects your license from compliance risks

<Callout type="warning">
**Why compliance matters:** A single violation can result in fines ($5k-$50k), license suspension, or even revocation. Sentinel helps you avoid these risks automatically.
</Callout>

## Key Features

### Pre-Flight Content Checks
Before any marketing goes live:
- **Validates claims** (no medical claims, no targeting minors)
- **Checks visuals** (no cartoon characters, bright colors, candy appeal)
- **Reviews language** (approved terminology, state-specific rules)
- **Verifies disclaimers** (required warnings, age gates)

### State-Specific Rules
Sentinel knows the rules for all 50+ markets:
- **California**: No health claims, strict packaging rules
- **Colorado**: No action figures or imagery attractive to children
- **New York**: Social equity requirements, strict advertising limits
- **Massachusetts**: No billboards within 500ft of schools
- **And 47+ more states**

### Ongoing Monitoring
- **Daily scans** of your website, social media, emails
- **Flags violations** before regulators see them
- **Suggests fixes** for non-compliant content
- **Tracks changes** in regulations (updates weekly)
