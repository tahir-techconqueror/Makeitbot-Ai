# Ledger - Pricing Strategist

Your AI pricing strategist who optimizes product prices, protects margins, and maximizes revenue using competitive intelligence and market data.

## What Ledger Does

**Ledger is your pricing expert** who:
- Analyzes competitor pricing in real-time
- Recommends optimal prices for each product
- Protects your profit margins
- Forecasts revenue impact of price changes
- Identifies price gaps and opportunities
- Automates dynamic pricing strategies

<Callout type="success">
**Why pricing matters:** A 1% improvement in price optimization can increase profits by 10%. Ledger helps you find that 1% across your entire catalog.
</Callout>

## Key Features

### Competitive Price Analysis
- **Real-time tracking** of competitor prices
- **Price positioning** (premium, value, or mid-tier)
- **Gap identification** (where you're over/under-priced)
- **Market benchmarks** by category and strain

### Margin Protection
- **Minimum margin enforcement** (never go below X%)
- **Cost-plus analysis** (ensuring profitability)
- **Volume-margin optimization** (trade-offs between volume and margin)
- **Loss leader identification** (strategic low prices)

### Revenue Forecasting
- **Price elasticity modeling** (how demand changes with price)
- **Revenue impact predictions** before price changes
- **Scenario analysis** ("what if we drop prices 10%?")
- **A/B test recommendations**

### Dynamic Pricing Rules
- **Time-based pricing** (happy hour, weekend specials)
- **Inventory-based pricing** (discount slow movers, premium for low stock)
- **Competitor-reactive pricing** (match or beat within X%)
- **Market condition triggers** (holiday, 420, special events)

## Pricing Strategies

### Premium Strategy
**Best for**: Established brands, superior quality, loyal customers

**Ledger's Approach:**
- Price in top 20% of market
- Focus on margin over volume
- Emphasize quality differentiation
- Avoid discounting (dilutes brand)

**Example:**
- Market Average: $30
- Your Price: $38 (+27%)
- Lower volume, but 55% margins

### Value Strategy
**Best for**: New brands, high-volume focus, price-sensitive markets

**Ledger's Approach:**
- Price in bottom 30% of market
- Maximize volume and market share
- Accept thinner margins (30-35%)
- Compete aggressively on price

**Example:**
- Market Average: $30
- Your Price: $25 (-17%)
- Higher volume, 32% margins

### Match Market Strategy
**Best for**: Mid-market brands, competitive balance

**Ledger's Approach:**
- Stay within Â±5% of market average
- Adjust based on quality and features
- Balance volume and margin
- React quickly to competitor moves

**Example:**
- Market Average: $30
- Your Price: $29-$31
- Moderate volume, 40% margins

## Best Practices

<Callout type="tip">
**Test incrementally**: Don't change 100 prices at once. Test 10-20 products, measure impact, scale if successful.

**Protect margins**: Set minimum margins in dynamic pricing rules. Never go unprofitable.

**Communicate changes**: If raising prices, explain why (quality, demand, market conditions). If lowering, create urgency (limited time, sale event).

**Monitor competitors weekly**: Prices change fast in cannabis. Stay vigilant with Radar + Ledger.
</Callout>

## Common Pricing Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Pricing Too Low
**Problem**: Leaving money on table, thin margins

**Fix**: Ledger identifies underpriced products, suggests increases

**Example**: You price at $25, market is $32, margins only 20%
- Recommendation: Raise to $30, margins improve to 40%

### Mistake 2: Ignoring Competitors
**Problem**: Overpriced vs. market, losing sales

**Fix**: Radar tracks competitors, Ledger adjusts accordingly

**Example**: Competitor drops price, you don't notice for 2 weeks
- Lost revenue: $800
- Fix: Enable competitor-reactive pricing rules

### Mistake 3: One-Size-Fits-All Pricing
**Problem**: Same margin target for all products

**Fix**: Different strategies by category

**Example**:
- Flower: 45% margin (high competition)
- Concentrates: 55% margin (premium, lower competition)
- Accessories: 65% margin (convenience pricing)
