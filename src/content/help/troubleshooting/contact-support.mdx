## When to Contact Support

### Immediate Support Needed

**Contact support immediately for:**

**Critical issues:**
- ‚ùå Can't access account (business halted)
- ‚ùå Payment processing down
- ‚ùå POS sync stopped (inventory out of sync)
- ‚ùå Campaign sent to wrong audience
- ‚ùå Data loss or corruption

**Use:** In-app chat or phone (if Premium)

### Same-Day Support

**Contact within hours for:**

**Important but not critical:**
- Feature not working as expected
- Error messages blocking work
- Integration issues
- Billing discrepancies
- Performance problems (slow loading)

**Use:** In-app chat or email

### Next-Business-Day

**Can wait for:**

**General questions:**
- How to use a feature
- Best practices guidance
- Feature requests
- General troubleshooting

**Use:** Email or Help Center

## Escalation Process

### If Issue Not Resolved

**Level 1:** Support team member
- Handles 95% of issues
- Can escalate if needed

**Level 2:** Senior support engineer
- Complex technical issues
- Integration problems
- Performance issues

**Level 3:** Development team
- Bug fixes
- Feature modifications
- System issues

**Escalation happens automatically** when needed
