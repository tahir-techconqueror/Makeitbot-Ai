## Types of Bundles

### 1. Category Bundles
**What it is:** Products from same category at a discount

**Examples:**
- **Flower Sampler**: 3√ó 3.5g strains (Indica, Sativa, Hybrid) - $80 (save $15)
- **Vape Variety Pack**: 3√ó cartridges (different strains) - $120 (save $20)
- **Edible Explorer**: Gummies + chocolate + beverage - $60 (save $12)

**Best for:**
- New customers (trying multiple products)
- Experienced users (stocking up)

### 2. Use-Case Bundles
**What it is:** Products designed for specific needs/occasions

**Examples:**
- **Sleep Bundle**: Indica flower + melatonin gummies + lavender tincture - $75
- **Social Bundle**: Sativa pre-rolls + mints + disposable vape - $65
- **Wellness Bundle**: CBD tincture + topical cream + bath salts - $85
- **Party Pack**: Edibles + pre-rolls + vape cart - $100

**Best for:**
- Solving customer problems
- Educational positioning

### 3. New Customer Bundles
**What it is:** Beginner-friendly starter packs

**Examples:**
- **Cannabis 101 Kit**: Low-THC flower (1g) + edible (5mg) + instruction guide - $30
- **First-Timer Pack**: Pre-roll + gummies + breath mints - $35
- **Try Everything**: Mini sizes of 5 product categories - $50

**Best for:**
- First-time buyers
- Reducing purchase anxiety
- Building loyalty early

### 4. Value Bundles (Clearance)
**What it is:** Moving slow inventory with aggressive discounts

**Examples:**
- **Weekly Deals Bundle**: 3 slow movers - $60 (30% off)
- **Mystery Box**: 5 random products - $50 (value $80+)
- **Overstock Special**: High-volume items - 40% off

**Best for:**
- Clearing old inventory
- Surprise/delight customers
- Creating urgency

### 5. Premium/Gift Bundles
**What it is:** High-end curated experiences

**Examples:**
- **Connoisseur Collection**: Top-shelf flower + live resin + premium edibles - $200
- **Executive Gift Set**: Luxury packaging, premium products - $150
- **Anniversary Pack**: Best products from year, special packaging - $175

**Best for:**
- Gift giving (holidays, birthdays)
- VIP customers
- Higher margins

## Creating Bundles in Markitbot

### Step 1: Navigate to Bundles

1. Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí Products ‚Üí Bundles**
2. Click **"Create Bundle"**

### Step 2: Configure Bundle Details

**Bundle Name:**
- Clear and descriptive
- Include benefit or use case
- Examples: "Sleep Starter Pack", "Party Bundle", "Cannabis 101 Kit"

**Bundle Description:**
Write compelling copy:
```
The Sleep Starter Pack helps you wind down after
a long day. Includes:

üåô Grandaddy Purple 3.5g (25% THC Indica)
üç¨ Melatonin + THC Gummies (10mg, 10-pack)
üíß Lavender CBN Tincture (500mg)

Perfect for: Nighttime relaxation, insomnia, stress relief

Save $18 vs. buying separately!
```

**Bundle Image:**
- Professional photo of products together
- Use lifestyle imagery (products in context)
- Show packaging if gift bundle

### Step 3: Select Products

Add products to bundle:

**Strategies:**
1. **Complementary Products**
   - Flower + grinder + rolling papers
   - Vape cart + battery + case

2. **Problem-Solution Stacks**
   - Pain relief: Topical + tincture + Indica
   - Energy boost: Sativa flower + mints + beverage

3. **Cross-Category**
   - Introduce customers to new categories
   - Flower buyer ‚Üí Add edibles or vape

4. **Fast Mover + Slow Mover**
   - Pair bestseller with product that needs movement
   - Bestseller drives bundle sales, slow mover gets exposure

**Example Bundle Configuration:**
```
Products:
1. Grandaddy Purple 3.5g - $35 (regular price)
2. Melatonin Gummies 10-pack - $28 (regular price)
3. Lavender Tincture 500mg - $30 (regular price)

Total Individual Price: $93
Bundle Price: $75
Customer Saves: $18 (19% off)
Your Margin: 38% (vs. 42% on individuals)
```

### Step 4: Set Bundle Pricing

**Pricing Strategies:**

**Percentage Discount:**
- 10-15% off: Standard bundle discount
- 20-25% off: Aggressive bundle (move inventory)
- 30%+ off: Clearance or loss leader

**Dollar Amount Off:**
- "Save $20" (easier for customers to understand)
- Round numbers ($75 instead of $73.50)

**Value Pricing:**
- "$100 value for $75" (emphasize savings)
- Position as deal

**Margin Protection:**
- Set minimum bundle price (maintain profitability)
- Example: Don't go below 30% total margin

**Tiered Pricing:**
- Buy 2 bundles: Additional 5% off
- Buy 3 bundles: Additional 10% off

### Step 5: Configure Availability

**Stock Management:**
- **Fixed quantity**: 50 bundles available
- **Unlimited**: As long as components in stock
- **Limited time**: Available until date/time

**Visibility:**
- **Public**: Visible to all customers
- **VIP only**: Exclusive for loyalty members
- **Hidden**: Direct link only (email campaigns)

### Step 6: Add Bundle to Store

- **Feature on homepage**: Promote prominently
- **Create landing page**: Dedicated bundle page
- **Add to navigation**: "Shop Bundles" menu item
- **Tag for search**: "bundle", "deal", "value pack"

**Test 1: Discount Amount**
- Bundle A: 15% off ($79)

- Winner: Bundle B (34% higher conversion)

**Test 2: Product Mix**
- Bundle A: Flower + edibles + tincture
- Bundle B: Flower + edibles + vape
- Winner: Bundle B (vape more popular)

**Test 3: Naming**
- Bundle A: "Sleep Bundle"

- Option A: "$75 (save $18)"

## Bundle Best Practices

<Callout type="tip">
**Start simple**: Create 3-5 bundles, test performance, then expand

**Update seasonally**: Summer (Sativas, outdoor), Winter (Indicas, cozy)

**Listen to customers**: If they frequently buy items together, bundle them

**Use bundles to educate**: "First-Time Buyer Pack" reduces anxiety, builds trust
</Callout>

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

**Mistake 1: Discount Too Small**
‚ùå 5% off bundle (not compelling)
‚úÖ 15-20% off (clear value)

**Mistake 2: Random Products**
‚ùå Flower + topical + beverage (no connection)
‚úÖ Flower + grinder + papers (natural combo)

**Mistake 3: All Slow Movers**
‚ùå 3 products no one wants
‚úÖ 1 bestseller + 2 slow movers (bestseller drives sales)

**Mistake 4: Too Many Options**
‚ùå 20 different bundles (decision paralysis)
‚úÖ 5-7 focused bundles (easier to choose)

**Mistake 5: Poor Descriptions**
‚ùå "Bundle #3"
‚úÖ "Sleep Starter Pack - Fall Asleep Faster"
