# Creating Product Carousels

Showcase products dynamically with carousels. Create curated collections that rotate on your homepage, category pages, or marketing campaigns to drive discovery and sales.

## What is a Product Carousel?

**A carousel is a rotating product showcase:**
- Multiple products displayed in a scrollable row
- Auto-rotates or manual swipe/click
- Curated collections (featured products, new arrivals, bestsellers)
- Dynamic or static (updates automatically or manually)
- Used on homepage, category pages, emails, social media

## Carousel Best Practices

### Product Selection

**Featured carousel (manual):**
- 6-12 products (not too many)
- Mix of categories (flower, vapes, edibles)
- Include best sellers + new products
- Balance price points
- Refresh weekly

**Bestsellers (automatic):**
- 8-15 products
- Last 30 days (not too short, not too old)
- Minimum 10-20 sales (don't show slow movers)
- Update daily

**New arrivals (automatic):**
- Last 14-30 days
- Maximum 12 products (don't overwhelm)
- Remove after 30 days (no longer "new")

### Design & Layout

**Mobile-first:**
- 1-2 products visible on mobile
- Easy swipe navigation
- Large tap targets for buttons
- Fast loading

**Desktop:**
- 4-5 products visible
- Arrow navigation + dots
- Hover effects (zoom, quick view)

**Product cards:**
- High-quality images (800x800px min)
- Clear pricing (OTD)
- Star ratings (social proof)
- "Add to Cart" button
- Stock status (if low)

### Auto-Rotation

**Settings:**
- Speed: 5-7 seconds per product
- Pause on hover (desktop)
- Pause on tap (mobile)
- Manual navigation always available
- Resume after 3 seconds of inactivity

**Don't:**
- Rotate too fast (< 3 seconds)
- Prevent manual navigation
- Auto-rotate on product pages (distracting)

### Performance

**Optimize loading:**
- Lazy load images (load as visible)
- Compress images (< 100KB each)
- Limit products (12 max per carousel)
- Cache carousel data (1 hour)

**Measure:**
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Products viewed
- Add-to-cart rate
- Revenue per carousel
