# Customer Segmentation

Target the right customers with the right message. Learn how to segment your audience for personalized marketing that drives higher engagement and sales.

## Why Segment Customers?

**Generic marketing vs. targeted:**

**Generic (one message to all):**
```
Email: "Check out our products!"
Open rate: 18%
Click rate: 2.1%
Conversion: 0.8%
```

**Segmented (personalized message):**
```
Email: "Sarah, your favorite Sativas are back!"
Open rate: 42%
Click rate: 8.4%
Conversion: 3.2%

4x better results
```

<Callout type="success">
**Real impact:** Segmented campaigns generate 3.2x higher revenue per recipient vs. non-segmented campaigns, with 68% better conversion rates.
</Callout>

## Best Practices

<Callout type="tip">
**Start simple:** Begin with RFM segmentation (auto-generated), then expand to custom segments.

**Test and learn:** A/B test messages to different segments to find what works.

**Don't over-segment:** 5-10 active segments is plenty. Too many becomes unmanageable.

**Keep segments fresh:** Auto-refresh daily so segments stay accurate.

**Combine strategies:** Use RFM + product preference + behavior for precision targeting.
</Callout>
