### Step 3: Choose Date & Time

**Best days:**
- **Thursday-Saturday**: Highest attendance
- **Avoid Mondays**: Lowest traffic
- **Holidays**: Check local calendar

**Best times:**
- **Afternoon events**: 2-5pm (weekend)
- **Evening events**: 6-9pm (weekday)
- **All-day events**: 10am-8pm (420, holidays)

**Lead time:**
- Small event (vendor day): 2-3 weeks notice
- Medium event (launch party): 4-6 weeks notice
- Large event (420): 8-12 weeks notice

## Event Best Practices

<Callout type="tip">
**Start small**: First event? Keep it simple. Vendor day or small tasting.

**Over-communicate**: Send multiple reminders. No-show rates are 20-40% even with RSVP.

**Capture data**: Collect emails/phones at check-in for follow-up.

**Make it exclusive**: Limited spots, RSVP required â†’ creates urgency.

**Post about it**: Share photos/videos during and after â†’ builds FOMO for next event.
