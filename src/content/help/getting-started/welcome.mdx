## What is Markitbot?

Markitbot is a multi-agent platform that helps cannabis businesses:

- **Automate Marketing** - Let AI handle your campaigns, emails, and SMS
- **Serve Customers 24/7** - AI budtender answers product questions instantly
- **Track Competitors** - Real-time competitive intelligence and pricing
- **Analyze Performance** - Deep analytics on sales, customers, and trends
- **Stay Compliant** - Automatic compliance checks for all content
- **Integrate POS** - Sync your menu with Alleaves, Dutchie, or Jane

<Callout type="tip">
Markitbot keeps customers in **your funnel** while routing orders to retail partners for fulfillment. No marketplace fees, no losing customers to competitors.
</Callout>
