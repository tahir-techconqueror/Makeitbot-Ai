# Navigating the Inbox

Your unified inbox is mission control for Markitbot. See all AI agent outputs, approve campaigns, review insights, and manage artifacts in one centralized location.

## What is the Inbox?

**Your inbox is where AI agents deliver:**
- Marketing campaigns (ready to approve)
- Competitive intelligence reports
- Product bundles and carousels
- Customer insights and analytics
- Compliance reviews
- Pricing recommendations
- SEO optimizations
- Event promotions

**Think of it as:** Your personal assistant's desk where they drop completed work for your review.

<Callout type="success">
**Average time saved:** 12 hours/week by having all agent outputs in one organized inbox vs. scattered across email, Slack, and spreadsheets.
</Callout>

## Taking Action on Inbox Items

### Approving a Campaign

**Step-by-step:**
1. Click inbox item to open
2. Review campaign details
3. Click **Preview** to see email/SMS
4. Click **Approve & Send** or **Schedule**
5. Confirm send time
6. Campaign moves to "Scheduled" status

**After approval:**
- Campaign sends at scheduled time
- You receive confirmation email
- Results appear in Analytics dashboard

### Editing Before Approval

**To modify a campaign:**
1. Open inbox item
2. Click **Edit Campaign**
3. Make changes (subject, content, audience)
4. Click **Save Changes**
5. Then **Approve & Send**

### Applying Recommendations

**For pricing/product changes:**
1. Open recommendation
2. Review suggested changes
3. Click **Apply All** or **Apply Selected**
4. Changes take effect immediately
5. Item marked as completed

### Dismissing Items

**To clear inbox without action:**
1. Click item checkbox
2. Click **Mark as Read** or **Archive**
3. Item moves to archived view

**Note:** Dismissed items can be recalled from Archive

## Inbox Best Practices

<Callout type="tip">
**Check daily:** Review inbox each morning to approve campaigns and act on opportunities.

**Prioritize actions:** Focus on "Action Needed" items first (campaigns, compliance alerts).

**Don't ignore insights:** Even if you don't act immediately, review reports to stay informed.

**Archive completed:** Keep inbox clean by archiving items you've addressed.

**Set up notifications:** Enable alerts for urgent items so you don't miss time-sensitive opportunities.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Inbox empty but expecting item

**Possible causes:**
1. Item still processing (agents can take 5-15 min)
2. Filtered view hiding item
3. Already archived

**Solutions:**
- Wait 15 minutes and refresh
- Click **[All]** filter to see everything
- Check **Archive** for accidentally dismissed items

### Issue: Can't approve campaign

**Possible causes:**
1. Campaign already sent
2. Permission issue (not admin/super_user)
3. Campaign has errors

**Solutions:**
- Check campaign status (may be "Sent")
- Verify your user role in Settings
- Open campaign to see error messages

### Issue: Too many notifications

**Solution:**
```
Settings â†’ Notifications
- Disable non-urgent email notifications
- Switch to daily summary instead of immediate
- Keep SMS for urgent items only
```
