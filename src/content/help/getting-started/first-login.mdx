# Your First Login

Welcome to Markitbot! Here's what happens when you first sign in and how to get started quickly.

## What to Expect

**On your first login, you'll:**
1. Complete your profile
2. See the onboarding wizard
3. Set up your organization
4. Connect integrations (optional)
5. Meet your AI agents
6. Create your first product/campaign

**Time required:** 10-15 minutes

<Callout type="success">
**Quick start:** You can skip steps and come back later. The dashboard will show a progress checklist to guide you.
</Callout>

## What Happens Next?

**After completing setup:**

1. **Agent activation**
   - Agents start analyzing your data
   - First insights arrive within 24 hours
   - Campaigns can be created immediately

2. **Inbox notifications**
   - You'll receive agent outputs in inbox
   - Review and approve recommended actions
   - Track performance in analytics

3. **Continuous optimization**
   - Agents learn from your business
   - Recommendations improve over time
   - Automated workflows save hours weekly
