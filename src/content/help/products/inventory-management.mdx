## Inventory Sync Methods

### 1. POS Integration (Automatic)

**Best for:** Dispensaries with POS systems

**How it works:**
1. Products sold in POS
2. Inventory decrements in POS
3. **Markitbot syncs automatically** (every 15-60 min)
4. Online inventory updated
5. Out-of-stock products hidden

**Supported POS:**
- Alleaves (recommended for NY)
- Dutchie
- Jane
- Flowhub
- Treez

**Setup:**
```
Settings â†’ Integrations â†’ POS
1. Connect your POS
2. Enable "Sync Inventory"
3. Set sync frequency (15-60 min)
4. Done! Inventory auto-updates
```

### 2. Manual Updates

**Best for:** Brands without POS, small catalogs

**How to update:**

**Single product:**
1. Dashboard â†’ Products â†’ [Product]
2. Click "Edit"
3. Update "Stock Quantity"
4. Save

**Bulk update:**
1. Dashboard â†’ Products â†’ Inventory
2. Click "Bulk Edit"
3. Select products
4. Update quantities
5. Save all

**CSV import:**
1. Dashboard â†’ Products â†’ Import
2. Download template
3. Update quantities in spreadsheet
4. Upload CSV
5. Confirm changes

### 3. Order-Based Deduction

**Automatic inventory deduction:**

**When customer orders:**
1. Customer adds to cart
2. Inventory **reserved** (not yet decremented)
3. Customer completes checkout
4. Inventory **decremented**
5. Stock level updated

**If order cancelled:**
- Inventory **returned** to available
- Customer can't hold stock indefinitely

**Reserve duration:**
- Cart: 30 minutes hold
- Unpaid order: 24 hours hold
- After expiration: Returns to available

### Inventory Turnover

**Measure how fast inventory sells:**

**Formula:**
```
Inventory Turnover = Cost of Goods Sold / Average Inventory Value

Example:
COGS (annual): $480,000
Avg Inventory Value: $52,000
Turnover: 9.2x per year

Days in Inventory: 365 / 9.2 = 40 days
```

**Cannabis industry standards:**
- **Good**: 8-12x per year (30-45 days)
- **Excellent**: 12-15x per year (24-30 days)
- **Poor**: <6x per year (>60 days)

**Improve turnover:**
1. **Reduce slow movers** (discount or discontinue)
2. **Stock more A-products** (high sellers)
3. **Better forecasting** (match supply to demand)
4. **Faster restocking** (reduce lead times)
